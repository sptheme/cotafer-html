The Mekong Dragon Boutique Hotel

  • No. 134, Street 51, Beoung Keng Kang I, Chamkar Mon, Phnom Penh, Cambodia

The Mekong Dragon Hotel welcomes guests with an outdoor swimming pool, well-appointed rooms and free WiFi access. Phnom Penh International Airport is conveniently placed 9 km from the hotel.

Sub headline - Heading 5

Mekong Dragon Hotel is 1 km away from both Royal Palace Phnom Penh and Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum. The vibrant Riverfront Park is 2.1 km away. An airport transfer can be arranged at an additional cost.

Sub headline - Heading 6

Rooms are spacious and bright. Each is fitted with air conditioning, a mini-fridge and windows offering ample natural light. They also have a fresh bathroom equipped with a hairdryer and toiletries.

Mekong Dragon Hotel houses a 24-hour front desk and an outdoor terrace where you can enjoy a relaxing cup of tea. It also offers traditional massages and laundry services. You can also rent a bicycle or a car to explore the area.
